Profile

I’m Benjamin Hollway, a front-end developer from Brighton. I’m a Christian, lover of music, and coffee addict. I thrive on taking your ideas and making them a reality.

For me, writing code began at the age of 8 and from there I turned web development into my full time job. I’ve been fortunate to work with companies such as Built by Buffalo, Metropoly, Antlab, and Agile Pixel.

I’m passionate about working with small businesses and startups, and giving them the boost they need to launch their product into the market. I enjoy working with new web technologies and libraries, and aim to bring this into each project.

Ocassionally, I speak at events such as The Web is or on podcasts like Unfinished Business.

When I’m not creating new websites, I’ll either be cycling or listening to and creating music.

If you’d like me to work alongside you with your next project, get in touch.
