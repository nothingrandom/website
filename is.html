My name is Benjamin Hollway, and I'm a freelance front-end developer based in Brighton, UK.

I write beautiful, intuitive code which solves everyday problems.

I first began to write code at the tender age of 8. I'm now 16, so I've been at it for a while now. During those years, I've continued to work at programming and have learnt multiple languages, such as HTML & CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Ruby. I've also worked on some fantastic projects, with great people.

I love crafting new projects and working with startups. I enjoy learning new web technologies and architectures. Whenever I work on something new, I try to bring something fresh and creative to the project.

I've had the joy of working alongside other web development agencies such as AntLab and Built by Buffalo.

While I'm not at my desk, you'll find me at the library, going on a short run, or listening to and creating music.

I'm always looking to work on new projects, so feel free to get in touch, and I'll aim to reply the same day.
