GotchaKnot

GotchaKnot is a small UK based company that sells handcrafted buggy handles. The handles encourage children to walk next to the pram without the parents loosing them.

Date

March 2014

Role

Design, Development,
Branding & Logo

URL

Simplicity

The idea of the GotchaKnot website is that it is as simple and easy to use as possible. The main users will be mothers of young children, so they don't have time for flashy animations and a complicated UI. Most of them will want to be able to purchase the item as quickly and hassle free as possible. With this in mind, I designed the website to be the most simplistic and straight forward as possible. As I am a good friends with this particular client, it is definatly a project I will revisit and redesign in the near future.
