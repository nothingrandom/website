Net Mag Design Challenge

After being nominated for a net award, I was asked to participate in the magazine's design challenge for that month. It was a great publicity booster, and an exciting project to work on.

Date

January 2014

Role

Design Mockup

URL

www.scottpatience.co.uk

The Brief

"This month weâ€™d like you to design a website for a visual artist. Artists typically want their site to display their work, communicate information about upcoming exhibitions, sell prints and/or books, carry a bio/about page, a media appearances or news page, and sometimes a blog."

The Concept

I wanted the website to focus on the artist, and their artwork with minimum distraction. I decided that I should make full usage of the screen to display both information about the artist and wide images. I also wanted to optimize for a smaller screen, so that they don't miss out on the experience. After deciding to display large pictures of each piece of work, I also made the choice to display information about the piece when the image is hovered over. This allows a certain element of elegance to exist on the website, which further adds to the creativity of the artist.

The Launch

After the design concept had been published in issue 252, I proceed to turn it into a working website. I launched the finished website a couple of months later, with a few tweaks to how it looked and worked.
