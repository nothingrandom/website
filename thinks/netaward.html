Net Award Nomination

Date

30th January, 2014

Reading time

1 minute

Category

News

Crazy things are happening

The net awards are a yearly award ceremony for web design and development. They often showcase some of the big names. This year that, among others, includes the Dropbox and Twitter design team in the "Team of the Year" catagory. Some amazing people have been nominated for a wide variety of catagories. One of these catagories is the "Emerging Talent of the Year".

Earlier in January the nomination stage opened. I saw a tweet about the awards and thought I'd check it out. Roughly 30 minutes after I noticed that the net awards were a thing, and I saw this tweet. I thought there was nothing to loose, so I asked to be nominated, and it appears that a few people did.

Then on Tuesday afternoon the voting opened. And there was my name, in black and white. (Ok, it's gold and white really...) A massive thank you to everyone who nominated me! If you feel like voting for me, you can view my page and the other nominations here.

Update 26/2/14

Since the awards began, I've also participated in the Net Mag Design Challenge.

Update 31/3/14

I've been shortlisted in my catagory.

Update 13/5/14

Jen Lamere won the Emerging Talent catagory. It was great to be nominated and shortlisted, and I'd like to thank everyone who took the time to vote for me.
