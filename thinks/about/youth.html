Children and Technology

4th Feb. 2014

Picture this, you are travelling on the bus or train and a few seats over from you is a young parent and their child. The child is between the ages of 3 and 5. The child begins to cry. Much to your surprise, the parent gets out their smart phone, launches a basic game, hands it to the child and then... silence. The child stops crying.
